    Candidates occupying a seat in DASA-2023 are NOT eligible for CSAB-2023 Special Round. Such candidates MUST SURRENDER / WITHDRAW / EXIT the DASA-2023 seats within the specified date (please refer to the schedule available in the DASA-2023 website) in order to be eligible for CSAB-2023 Special Round.

    Paying the seat acceptance fee is mandatory for all three Willingness (FLOAT/SLIDE/FREEZE) submitted by candidates. Failure to pay the seat acceptance fee by the deadline will result in the cancellation of the provisionally allotted seat, and the candidate will not be considered for seat allocation in the subsequent rounds.

    INTRODUCTION

    The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) 2023 has been set up by the Ministry of Education [erstwhile Ministry of Human Resources Development (MHRD)] to manage and regulate the joint seat allocation for admissions to 118 institutes for the academic year 2023-24. This includes 23 IITs, 31 NITs, IIEST Shibpur, 26 IIITs and 38-Other Technical Institutes Funded Fully or Partilly by Central or State Government (Other-GFTIs). Admission to all the academic programs offered by these Institutes will be made through a single platform.

    All candidates having a provisionally allocated seat in the NIT+ system (allocated in any round of JoSAA-2023 and not been cancelled or withdrawn) and having paid Seat Acceptance Fee (SAF) are also required to pay the Partial Admission Fee (PAF) online from their JoSAA portal during 29 July – 31 July 2023 (5:00pm) 01 Aug 2023 (11:59 pm). Non-payment of the PAF within the stipulated time will lead to cancellation of the allocated seat.

    Candidates whose category was changed from OBC-NCL/GEN-EWS/SC/ST to GEN category during Document Verification in JoSAA Rounds due to non-submission of a valid category certificate may request restoration of their original category status (OBC- NCL/GEN-EWS/SC/ST) before registering in CSAB-Special Rounds at https://nitrkl.ac.in/csab2023-crrp to restore the category latest by 31 July 2023 (17:00 hrs.)
