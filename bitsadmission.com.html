Welcome to BITS, Pilani's Admissions website!

This web site describes the admission modalities for the on-campus degree programmes offered at Pilani campus, Goa Campus and Hyderabad Campus of BITS, Pilani.

Direct admission to Board topper



The application form link for admissions under the "Board Topper" scheme will be open from May 15, 2024, to June 15, 2024. During this period, candidates who secured first rank (Board topper) in PCM/PCB in their respective boards are eligible to apply for admissions. Click Here to see more details.
The first rank holder of PCM stream will be considered for admission to all First Degree courses in BITS Pilani.
The first rank holder of PCB stream will be considered for admission to B. Pharmacy programme only.
